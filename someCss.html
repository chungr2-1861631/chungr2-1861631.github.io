Pembroke Welsh Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a cattle herding dog breed which originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales. The Pembroke is the more popular breed between Pembroke Welsh Corgi and Cardigan Welsh Corgi, and the fact that Queen Elizabeth II preferred the Pembroke probably plays into it. Source: Wikipedia

The corgi is one of the smallest herding dogs. Source: Wikipedia
